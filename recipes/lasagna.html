Lasagna

Description

Waiting for the lasagna to come out of the oven is the hardest part of this simple recipe.

Ingredients

    1 pound ground beef
    1 jar (26 oz.) pasta sauce
    1 container (15 oz.) ricotta cheese
    2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    1/2 cup grated parmesean cheese
    2 large eggs
    12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained

Steps

    Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
    Brown ground beef in 12 inch skillet and drain
    Stir in pasta sauce until heated through
    Combine ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, 1/4 cup parmesean cheese, and eggs in a large bowl
    Evenly spread 1 cup of meat sauce in a 13 x 9 baking dish
    Arrange 4 lasagna noodles lengthwise over the sauce
    Top noodles with 1 cup of meat sauce and half of the ricotta cheese mixture
    Repeat, ending with sauce
    Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes
    Remove foil and sprikle with 1/4 cup parmesean cheese
    Bake uncovered for another 5 minutes
    Let stand for 10 minutes before serving
